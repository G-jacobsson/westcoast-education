About us

WestCoast Education is an educational company located on the west coast just north of Gothenburg. We have been in the education industry for almost 40 years. Our business goal is, and has always been, to provide technical training in the IT field. We have been very successful over the years. We have specialised particularly in systems development training for web and mobile solutions.
